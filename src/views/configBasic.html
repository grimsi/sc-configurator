Fullscreen Mode:

Sets the mode at which the game is displayed on your screen. Window Borderless Window Fullscreen

Resolution:

Sets the Resolution at which the game is rendered. 320×240 480×320 640×480 800×480 1024×600 1280×720 1024×768 1366×768 1200×900 1440×900 1920×1080 1920×1200 2560×1440 2560×1600 3840×2160 4096×2160 Custom
X-Resolution × Y-Resolution
FPS Limit: Sets the maximum FPS at which the game is rendered. Set to 0 for unlimited FPS.

Vertical Sync

Toggles V-Sync. V-Sync prevents tearing.

Field of View:

Sets the ingame FOV. A higher value lets the game display more content on your screen, but may result in lower FPS.

Mouse Acceleration:

Sets the mouse acceleration. Higher value means higher acceleration.

Mouse Smoothing:

Sets the mouse smoothing. Higher value means more smoothing.

Texture Quality:

Sets the resolution of the textures used in the game. Very Low Low Medium High Very High Ultra

Shadow Quality:

Sets the resolution of the shadows rendered ingame. Very Low Low Medium High Very High Ultra

Shadow Blur:

Sets the blurriness of the shadows rendered ingame. Higher value means more blur.

Ambient Occlusion:

Sets the quality of SSDO. Low Medium High

Anti-Aliasing:

Sets the quality of Anti-Aliasing. No AA FXAA SMAA MSAA

Anisotropic Filtering:

Sets the quality of Anisotropic Filtering. 2x 4x 8x 16x

Sharpening:

Sets the sharpness of the game. Higher value means sharper image.

Motion Blur:

Sets the motion blur. Higher value means more motion blur.

Depth of Field:

Sets the depth of field. Higher value means more blur.

Radial Blur:

Sets the radial blur. Higher value means more radial blur.

HDR Brightness:

Sets the brightness in HRD Mode. Higher value means brighter image.

HDR Bloom:

Sets the bloom in HRD Mode. Higher value means more bloom.

HUD Headbobbing:

Sets the headbobbing of the HUD. Higher value means more headbobbing.

Chromatic Aberration:

Sets the chromatic aberration value. Higher value means more chromatic aberration.

Reflection Quality:

Sets the quality of reflections. Low Medium High Very High

Color Grading

Toggles Color Grading. Turning it off may result in higher FPS but also in duller colors.
